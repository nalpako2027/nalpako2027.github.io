# Professional Consultation Services
I provide rigorous statistical and algorithmic consultation frameworks designed to keep models honest and maximize commercial or academic ROI. 

## 🛠️ Specialized Consultation Services

### 1. Causal Inference & Selection Bias Correction
*   **Experimental Research Design:** Designing end-to-end Randomized Controlled Trials (A/B testing architectures) from scratch—ensuring true randomization, calculating sample sizes (power analysis), establishing rigorous control metrics, and accounting for threats to validity.
*   **A/B Testing Audits:** Evaluating existing experimental splits to detect flaws, isolate causal impact, and correct for hidden self-selection biases.
*   **Observational Data Modeling:** Utilizing Propensity Score Matching (PSM), Fixed-Effects Modeling, and Regression-Discontinuity Design to extract reliable insights when running randomized control trials is very costly (or unethical such as exposing students to bullying or exposing patients to a virus like COVID).

### 2. Predictive & Behavioral Modeling Pipelines
*   **Behavioral & Psychological Analytics:** Translating psychometric metrics (such as motivation, anxiety, attitude, dispositions and environmental factors like bullying) into structured predictive models to quantify their direct impacts on outcomes.
*   **Advanced Latent Variable Modeling:** Utilizing rigorous statistical frameworks to model hidden behavioral constructs and isolate complex interaction effects within human-centric datasets.
*   **Predictive Pipeline Engineering:** Building end-to-end Python/Stata analytics pipelines featuring custom logic validations and cross-validation checks to prevent model overfitting.


### 3. Academic & Methodological Advisory
*   **Statistical De-biasing:** Auditing research datasets to detect and resolve dangerous hidden pitfalls, such as unaccounted feature type and its operationalization, missing data, outliers or improper sampling distributions.
