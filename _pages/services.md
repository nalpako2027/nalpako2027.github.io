# Professional Consultation Services
I provide rigorous mathematical, statistical, and algorithmic consultation frameworks designed to keep models honest and maximize commercial or academic ROI. 

## 🛠️ Specialized Consultation Services

### 1. Causal Inference & Selection Bias Correction
*   **A/B Testing Audits:** Evaluating experimental splits to isolate true causal impact from self-selection artifacts.
*   **Observational Data Modeling:** Utilizing Propensity Score Matching (PSM), Instrumental Variables, and Difference-in-Differences to extract reliable insights when running randomized control trials is impossible.

### 2. Predictive Pipeline Engineering
*   **Custom Risk & Churn Modeling:** Engineering robust classification frameworks to identify high-risk customer or user behavior profiles.
*   **Algorithmic Architecture:** Building end-to-end Python/R pipelines with rigorous multi-model validation checks to prevent overfitting.

### 3. Academic & Methodological Advisory
*   **Statistical De-biasing:** Auditing research datasets to detect and resolve dangerous hidden pitfalls, such as missing data handling or improper sampling distributions.
